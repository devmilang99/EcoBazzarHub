# 🛍️ Eco-Bazzar

> A modern e-commerce platform that enables businesses to sell products and services online through powerful digital storefronts.

---

## 📱 Overview

**Eco-Bazzar** is a Flutter-based mobile e-commerce application designed to bridge businesses and customers through seamless digital commerce. From small vendors to growing enterprises, Eco-Bazzar empowers sellers to manage their online presence, process orders, and engage customers — all from a single platform.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏪 For Customers
- Browse and discover products across multiple vendors
- Smart product search and category filtering
- Add to cart, wishlist, and place orders with ease
- Real-time order tracking from placement to delivery
- Secure and streamlined checkout with payment integration

### 🧑‍💼 For Vendors / Businesses ( In Progress )
- Digital storefront setup and management
- Product catalog management (add, edit, remove listings)
- Vendor dashboard with sales analytics and insights
- Order management and fulfillment tracking
- Customer engagement and notification tools

### 🔧 Platform Highlights
- Secure payment workflows with multiple payment options
- Real-time order and inventory synchronization
- Cross-platform support for Android and iOS
- Scalable architecture designed for high-traffic retail environments
